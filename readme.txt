== Description ==

You just drag and drop to indicate where you want to place ads on your blog. There is nothing to modify, no HTML knowledge required, no template to edit,  nothing to integrate. Everything is done in a minute! It's that easy. Adwit Banner Manager will place your favorite advertising programs in the right location, without placement errors (wrong ad size, wrong display zone, special template...). Just let the system optimize the ad rotations and find the best random banner to maximize your revenues.

ABM manages and optimizes all types of advertising campaigns (Google Adsense, affiliate programs, direct advertising,…) You can use it for your other Websites by connecting to our more advanced Adwit-express pro manager. You will benefit from advanced live statistics and a wide range of ad formats (Text ads, Slides, In-text ads,…).

The Adwitserver technology is the simplest ad server to display ads on a blog with the latest ad targeting technology. With ABM, you not only simplify the setup and ad management of your blog, but you maximize your income from each advertising display zone. 

Features:

*   New: Ad manager with Multi Widgets optimization system for your banner
*   Starts ad quickly without requiring configuration
*   Easy to add ad slots with “drag and drop” by page type
*   Simple way to import all of your advertising programs, including Google ad sense, adify, adbrite, any affiliate advertisement, commission junction...
*   Real time stats
*   Widget included for your sidebar
*   Ad rotation optimization tool (banner rotator without configuration need)
*   Campaign manager lets you follow all your ad networks ads from a single point
*   Geotargeting and capping frequencies for all your ad banners
*   Customer service 24/7
*   Direct access to adwit-express ad manager (free ad optimization platform)
*   Adwit-local ad agency compatible so you can sell ads directly to advertisers
*   Free upload of image ad (banner) - Available on version 2.0.0
*   create customized zones to display your banners

== Installation ==


= Install files into your WP Plugins directory: =
1. Download adwit_banner_manager.zip to your local hard drive. 
2. Extract the files. 
3. FTP the adwit_banner_manager files into your WP Plugins directory.
4. Activate the plug-in through the 'Plugins' menu in WordPress

= My ad doesn't fit on my widget   =

* check if there is not extra paddings shifting your ad
